While You Are With Us We Offer You Different Things

Board Games

We have more than 1,000 Board games in our massive library wall. Choose a game or have one of our Game Gurus recommend you one for you and your Group. Whether you choose a Board Game or we recommend one, in either case we will bring the game out, set it up and explain all the rules, from A to Z. We make sure you are playing the game the right way. If you and your group wish we can even play a quick round with one of our Game Gurus. There are no rules, you can play as many games as you like and for as long as you for 99 SEK per person.

Food

Pasta is the heart of what we do. A choice of shapes from fusilli to campanelle to linguine and tagliatelle are made fresh using the finest durum wheat semolina in our café every single day. We also specialize in great tasty pizzas and a wide range of salads and homemade dolci. There is a way you can personalize your meal, handmade fresh pasta, pizza, sauces, dressings and dolci, the spacious, beautiful café design, the fact you sit where you like and stay as long as you want, eating delicious food and play fun games will create the laid back vibes we are know and loved for, and what makes Board Games Café *the* place to go.

Drinks

To complement your entire experience, we have wide range of amazing drinks, our crafted cocktails have been expertly designed and prepared by our mixologists, and our selection of wines have been picked to perfectly pair with your meals, we also have wide range of non-alcoholic drinks. We ‘re sure you have a favorite drink, but today why not treat yourself to something little different?
